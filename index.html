Our Organic Process

From farm to table, we maintain the highest standards at every step of production.

Organic Farming

We source peanuts from certified organic farms in Plateau State, free from synthetic pesticides.

Precision Roasting

Our peanuts are roasted at optimal temperatures to enhance flavor while preserving nutrients.

Cold-Press Extraction

Using traditional methods, we extract oil below 50°C to maintain nutritional integrity.

Eco-Packaging
